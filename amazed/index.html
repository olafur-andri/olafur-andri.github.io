Amazed is an Android game where you move a sphere around by tilting your device. Your task is to find out the layout of a maze you are stuck in but once you figure out the whole maze, you finish the level. Be careful, though. There's not a lot of space on each plane and if you fall down, you have to start the level again from the beginning. Also, watch out for traps.
