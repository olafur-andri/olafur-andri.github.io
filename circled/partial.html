CIRCLED

- A mobile game inspired by the always-classic Snake game

android

CIRCLED is an Android game that seeks to bring new life to Snake, a game made popular by Nokia phones shortly before the year 2000. You play as a Circle, or a series of Circles, that move across the level to eat Cookies. With every devoured Cookie, a new Circle is created and appended to the series of Circles. You may not hit the edges of the screen or yourself since that will start the game over.

extension

The game also provides some extended functionalities. Such as a local and global leaderboard and themes. Each different theme changes the way the game looks and therefore, how it feels. The leaderboards give you a chance to compete with yourself and others throughout the world to reach the highest attainable score.
