Privacy

All users of CIRCUM are prompted to agree to CIRCUM's Privacy Policy as a way to ensure that users are on top of the personal information gathered by the app, which are:

  • Third party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to the certain subpages of CIRCUM and websites on the Internet.
  • Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to you based on your visits to CIRCUM's subpages and Internet websites.
  • You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Ads Settings. Should you want to opt out of a third-party vendor's use of cookies for personlized advertising, visit www.aboutads.info.
  • The date and time of users achieving a new High-Score.
  • The distinct username users choose should they want to be a part of the global leaderboard.
